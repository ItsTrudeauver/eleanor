Survival Guide

Welcome to Norwager

Surviving requires you to not be a pickle. Do that much first. Some posh motherfuckers come by time to time thinking they can buy the place. Got them nowhere aside from either a hospital room or a coffin, and I ain't even had to do shit. You mix in, or stay out of trouble, or end up like them.

The only shit that matters

  • Cash: We run on cash. Cash cards work. None of that corpo cred, unless you want all of your sweet money to be unusable. Offshore accounts tolerated, but you'll draw eyes. Need the right vendors. Can't tell you who
  • Weapons: Get a gun. You don't have a choice. Shit's crazy at night. You don't bring a gun with you and they rob you blind. "They" could be me.
  • Food & Water: Stack your non-perishables. Seriously why would you even stay at Norwager. This place's a shit hole.

Who you need to know

Don't be snooping around if you don't have anyone to show you the ropes. If you don't know where to find these people, leave.

  • Memphis: That's me. Come get ya guns. They ain't cheap but they kill.
  • Madeleine: Knows a few motels. Quality not guaranteed. You meet her or live in your car. #1 info merchant
  • Traders: They sell stuff. Motherfuckers will charge you 2 grand for a pair of shoes. Don't let them rip you off, this ain't a supermarket. They're not real gangsters, don't let them catch you scared.

Quick Tips

  • Avoid unnecessary conflicts. Keep it lowkey.
  • If it's too good to be true then it's shit
  • Flaunt your cash lose your stash. Nobody makes the rules. Sell your fancy vest and put a button-up on.
