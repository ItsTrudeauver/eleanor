Ashway Net

Probably the only place with any real structure. North part is in Lumberton, South part Norwager. South is where the rats stay. And if the rats go north, the Lumberton police kick them back south.

    Some places you might wanna know:

  • Rusting depot: Get ya busses here. Can find some okay traders. Info never as good as Maddie's and for 10 times the price. Most of the time dont bother. Put your best boots on at night.
  • Chipped paint pub: Only called that because its got chipped paint. Solid drinks, dont eat. About the most normal place otherwise.
  • Industrial complex: Some factories. Nothing worth taking but they pay some if you got limbs for the work.

Low Road Lot

Where you keep your stash. If you're careful you're never gonna lose your shit. If you get fleeced, get good.

    Getting around:

  • Tunnel to 14th: Pay for your keys, get storage space. Stay lowkey, dont dump a bunch of shit in at once unless you want all of it gone.
  • Garage vault: Mechanic guy who holds dead drops. Can actually fix your rust bucket. Some connections but you gotta earn his trust
  • Overpass: Find me here. You get tailed and I shoot you. Or pay me and I shoot the motherfuckers behind you instead.
  • Scorched walls: Burnt down storage units, old corpo shit. Find your deals, dont get scammed.

Green Market

Like the name. Quality not guaranteed. Illegal or not who gives a shit.

    Map, kind of

  • Spice alley: Specializes in spice. You know what spice is. Dont OD. Black market shit are mostly fake. Cover your nose.
  • Garbage stalls: You know why we call it that. Stolen goods, some maybe yours, or garbage from other places. Dont believe the "vintage" bullshit.
  • Signal towers: Pay to get your calls outside. Your cellphone got no signal here. 30 a minute, no bargain.

Concrete rise

The uphill bit. Where your landlords are. Dont rent their shit.

    If you ever go there

  • Staircase alley: Leads up the rise, just go straight. Floods all the time. You can also stash your shit here if you got space or the shit aint important. Most likely wont last.
  • Back-Porch market: Tech. Cant test it dont buy it. If you get scammed nobody's snitching.

Golden Grove

Piece of shit place but its quiet. Book it as soon as motherfuckers in yellow jackets approach you. Thats some cult.

    Some spots

  • Lush halls: They actually got trees round this part. They set up fights there. Stay out of their business.
  • Shrine: The freaky cult shit. Leave them alone. They aint afraid of dying but they will try and get you in.
  • 1426 Bench: Low-level info roaches there. Dont bother.
